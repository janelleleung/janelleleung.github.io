JCL Immigration Outreach

Explore the company website @ JCL Immigration

Overview:

I worked as the Outreach Coordinator at JCL Immigration, an immigration law firm based in Scottsdale, Arizona. The firm works to help clients apply for visas across the globe from Taiwan to Pakistan to Mexico. During my time here, I completely redid all existing social media platforms on LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram. With my previous experience of working part time at a law firm (Davis Miles McGuire Gardner, PLLC) and background competing in speech competitions about modern American topics, I was able to create customized content whilst being considerate of the current political climate surrounding immigration.

JCL immigration’s website and features including Logo, color representation, and content were all used in perspective to the law firm’s values and visual identity.





Build:

Role: Project Manager
Tools: Illustrator, Photoshop, WordPress
Timeline: July 2020 – Present
JCL immigration’s website and features including Logo, color representation, and content were all used in perspective to the law firm’s values and visual identity.

“We enjoy practicing immigration law because I can help people realize their dreams of living in the U.S., leading a productive life, and raising their children in a safe environment where they can realize their potentials. ” -JCL Immigration



Brand Goals:

Trust and Transparency
It is essential that clients trust JCL Immigration as clients will be entrusting confidential and sensitive information with a goal to a certain immigration status.

Efficiency & Accountability
Immigration paperwork can take a time to go through. Thus, JCL Immigration does all it can to advance the process more quickly while performing precise work.

Overal Design:

• Professional and friendly vectors of people to represent the diverse client base JCL Immigration has
• Logo is representative of an agave plant that is native to Arizona
• Main colors of blue, white, and black to reflect trust, intelligence, and reliability
