Hug

Underwear Shopping App for All Women - UI/UX



Why+Problem:

Hug exists to help women find a bra and underwear based on accurate measurements down to the last centimeter, not just generic cup sizes. There is nnnecessary difficulty and uncomfort buying lingerie that is comfortable and there is a significant Asian American female population in the states that are able to buy underwear that fits and looks appealing overseas, but not within the States.



Introduction:

Meet Hug. Hug is the essential app to purchase womens bras, undies, and all things lingerires. By helping users measure their chest size and bust along with accurate feedback and research on bras, Hug helps you find underwear that feels the way it should: comfortable. In this body-positive and friendly community, this app helps solve a trivial problem that women face with ease. Hug will be there every step of the way to find the perfect lingerie for you. After all, as your unique self, one size fits you.

The App:


Home:

The purpose of the home screen is to provide a starting point to the app and highlight Hug’s latest work and products.The home screen is divided into four sections that can be adjusted as needed. These four sections are Specials, Featured Article, Just In, and a Spotlight on Hug’s Lead Researcher. Since this underwear app is created to be distinctly personal, comfortable, and approachable, consistently using gentle and light colors while offering preview pictures as buttons helped to cleanly organize the home page.








Onboarding Sign Up:

The onboarding sign up process allows the user to enter your clothing size and describe your ideal bra and underwear. It is meant to be a brief introduction that familiarizes the user with the gentle mood of the app and provide initial suggestions for bras and underwear that may best suit the user.



Shop:

To be consistent with the setup of the Home page, the Shop page is visually organized in a similar manner to allow for better learnability. The Shopping page is broken down into Just In, Custom Fit, Bras, Underwear, and Sale. To encourage users to shop, Hug specials and coupons are listed at the top of the page.






Wish List:

Providing an opportunity for users to save liked items into a list for later allows the user to keep track of items they may wish to purchase. Thus, the Wish List lets the user create a list of their choosing and add to any of their created lists on their own accord. A picture of their saved product is shown for quick reference and comparison.





Learn:

Once again, the Learn page is consistent with the Home and Shop page set up and provides Health and Hug Related articles to increase user trust and provide reliable information.







Profile:

The purpose of the profile page is to provide information about the user and is thus simplified to provide links to My Account, Order History, Inbox, App Settings, Help and Information, Find and Order, and Feedback. This page is the catch all to provide all the help users may need to smoothly navigate the Hug app.

Checkout:

The Shopping Bag icon is always visible to provide easy access to purchase products. To add an item to the Shopping Bag, all the user simply needs to do is press “Add to Bag” when viewing a product. Clicking on the Shopping Bag icon will display what is currently in the Shopping Bag, options to add a promo code or gift wrapping, and the estimated total price of the order. The Checkout button will take the user confirm their shipping address, shipping method, payment method, ordered items, and the complete order summary. After this information is reviewed, the “Submit Order” button will then tell the user that their order has been successfully placed.
