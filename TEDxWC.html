TEDxWellesleyCollege

Explore the nonprofit's website @ TED

Overview:

I worked as the treasurer of the TEDxWellesleyCollege, an event where several ideas and narratives are shared to inspire one another and spark innovative ideas. In coordination with Wellesley College and an all-female, diverse team, we worked together to create a unique event to celebrate the brilliance, inspirational ideas, and curiosity within the Wellesley College community and beyond. Along the way, I also took on the role of helping to design the TEDxWellesleyCollege website and sponsorship brochure. The sponsorship brochure is a presentation that is sent to companies to organizations that may choose to help sponsor the TEDxWellesleyCollege event. Thus, I chose to create personalized images that reflect Wellesley College’s natural campus beauty during spring, summer, fall, and winter. Furthermore, I ensured that I followed TEDx guidelines for logos and color schemes in order to establish the event’s credibility and alignment with TED.







Build:

Role: Treasurer + Designer
Tools: Illustrator, InDesign, HTML, CSS, Javascriptr
Timeline: March 2020 – Present

“At the end of the day, what you bring to the table will change the field that you’re in and no one can bring that except for you.” – Eugenia Duodu, CEO of Visions of Science Network for Learning (TEDxYouth@Toronto)



Brand Goals:

Credibility
Since Wellesley College has been approved to use the TED name and brand, it is important that TEDxWellesleyCollege remains consistent of TED’s values and guidelines in order to attract sponsors and audiences

Integrity and Respectability
Due to the collaboration between Wellesley College, a notable all women's college that attracts student across the globe and TED, a well-known organization known for its incredible speeches and ideas, it is essential that all content is precise, professional, and representative of the two brands since both have allowed a student run organization to use their names.

Overal Design:

• Professional and clean design to represent TED’s classic red and black color scheme and typography
• Wellesley College photography showcased to appeal to Wellesley College’s natural beauty that stems from its campus throughout the seasons
• Utilizing images from Wellesley College throughout the decades to exemplify Wellesley College's long history of sending out women into the world
